💡 Essential Info

Waste & recycling

🗑️ Bins & sorting

The bins are located in front of the gate.

Green bin — general waste (collected Monday and Thursday).

Yellow bin — plastics, cardboard, paper.

Glass bin — bottles and jars (collection point 200m away, towards the bakery).

Heating & air conditioning

🌡️ Thermostat

The thermostat is in the hallway, to the right of the door. Recommended: 20°C. Do not exceed 24°C.

❄️ Air conditioning

Remote on the living room coffee table. Use AUTO mode. Please turn off when you leave.

Hot water

🚿 Water heater

The water heater is in the utility closet in the hallway. It runs on automatic mode.

If you run out of hot water, check the dedicated circuit breaker (row 2, position 4 on the electrical panel).

Where to find things

🧺 Linen & textiles

Towels — in the bathroom cabinet drawers.

Extra blankets — top shelf of the wardrobe in bedroom 1.

Extra pillows — same wardrobe.

🔌 Useful appliances

Hair dryer — main bathroom drawer.

Iron — laundry room, top shelf.

Vacuum cleaner — utility closet in the hallway.

Practical tips

💡 Good to know

Wi-Fi works best in the living room and kitchen. The signal is weaker in bedroom 3.

The stove is induction: only use flat-bottomed magnetic cookware.

The living room shutter sometimes gets stuck — use the wall button (not the remote) to reset it.

The gate closes automatically after 30 seconds.
